# Adidas Revenue Dashboard


## Overview
Analysed an Adidas sales dataset covering 6 product lines across multiple 
US states and retail channels to uncover revenue trends, product performance, 
and market concentration risks.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (data cleaning)
- Power BI (dashboard & visualisation)

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue: $120.17M
- Total Profit: $47.22M
- Total Units Sold: 2M
- Total Products: 6

## Key Findings
- Men's Street Footwear and Women's Apparel are the strongest revenue drivers
- Revenue fluctuates seasonally — business is not consistently growing
- Online is the leading sales channel, followed by Outlet
- Revenue is heavily concentrated in New York, California and Florida

## Recommendations
- Launch targeted campaigns in the South and Midwest to close the regional gap
- Run promotions during slow months to reduce seasonal revenue dips
- Continue investing in the online channel as it has the most growth potential
- Review pricing and marketing strategy for Women's Athletic Footwear
- Expand into new state markets to reduce over-reliance on dominant locations..
